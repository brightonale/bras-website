### [2025-10-28 18:22:29+00:00](https://www.instagram.com/p/DQXOEhLiHRy/)

BRAS Halloween Special: THE DARK MILE 

Join us this Thursday at 7:00pm for our first official BRAS pub crawl!

Costumes are highly encouraged!

We’ll be taking on The Dark Mile, a Halloween-themed crawl along London Road. The idea is simple: visit a string of great pubs we don’t usually hit (since they don't usually serve real ale) and enjoy a stout in each one.

To complete The Dark Mile, you’ll need to have a stout at every stop - but remember, there’s no pressure to keep pace or to drink at every pub. Soft drinks and alternatives are absolutely fine.

To help pubs handle our orders smoothly, we’ll move on once a few have finished their drink. Naturally, some groups will move a bit faster - that’ll sort itself out during the night.

 The Route:

1. The Hare and Hounds - 75 London Rd, BN1 4JF (Our starting point at 7:00pm)

2. The World’s End - 60-61 London Rd, BN1 4JE

3. New Unity - 52-53 London Rd, BN1 4JX

4. Presuming Ed’s -  114-115 London Rd, BN1 4JG

5. Hobgoblin - 31 York Pl, BN1 4GU

6. The King & Queen - 13-17 Marlborough Pl, BN1 1UB

See you all on Thursday for THE DARK MILE.....🎃

### [2023-11-13 19:00:27+00:00](https://www.instagram.com/p/CzmOC64otwp/)

Welcome to The Brighton Real Ale Society (BRAS)!

Our society is based around the mutual love of real ales, which we explore every week by going to a different Brighton pub and ordering a round of a real ale. Then, we each rate the ale 1-10, and the resulting average is the BRAS’ score for that weeks ale. After the first round, you are free to drink whatever you’d like (just make sure it’s not lager haha). We also plan to take the society on brewery tours and go to some beer festivals, on top of our weekly pub socials.

Past being a group of ale lovers, we hope to be a fun and safe community for people to enjoy, meet some new people, and discover some new pubs and beers!

Feel free to contact us whenever and we'll be happy to answer any questions you may have. Cheers! 🍻
